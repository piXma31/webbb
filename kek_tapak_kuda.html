Kek Tapak Kuda

Ingredients:

  • 4 Grade A eggs / 5 Grade B eggs
  • 1/2 cup caster sugar
  • 1/2 cup self-raising flour
  • 1 tablespoon ovellete (emulsifier)
  • 3 teaspoons powdered milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla essence
  • 125 grams margarine/butter (melted)

How to Cook:

  • Beat all the ingredients above except for melted butter until fluffy and well risen.
  • Once fluffy, add the melted butter. Fold back until well combined or mix briefly.
  • Set aside 100g of the batter and mix in 1 tablespoon of cocoa powder if you want to make the cocoa color for the inner part. Save some excess for topping.
  • I use a 10-inch pan. Bake at 150°C for 25 to 30 minutes according to the suitability of each oven.
  • Once baked, let it cool slightly by flipping the cake.
  • Flip it back and cut it in half in the middle.
  • You'll get two pieces of cake from this batch. Wrap them tightly using plastic wrap.
