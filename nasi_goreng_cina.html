Nasi Goreng Cina Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 bowls of white rice
  • 2 eggs
  • Sliced chicken
  • Shrimp filling
  • Mixed vegetables (carrots, long beans, corn kernels)
  • Pepper to taste
  • 1 chicken bouillon cube
  • 1/2 teaspoon sesame oil
  • Some oil for stir-frying
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 2 shallots
  • Fried shallots
  • Sliced green onions

How to Cook:

  • Heat oil and add the roughly ground ingredients. Sauté until golden and fragrant.
  • Add the sliced chicken and stir until partially cooked, then add the shrimp filling.
  • Push some of the sautéed ingredients to the side of the pan and add the eggs. Stir the eggs slightly to make scrambled eggs, but don't make them too smooth. Let the eggs form into small lumps.
  • Crush the chicken bouillon cube and add it to the pan along with the pepper and mixed vegetables.
  • Add the white rice and sesame oil, then stir until the rice is well combined and slightly crispy.
  • Season with salt and sugar to taste. Be careful when adding salt because the chicken bouillon cube already adds saltiness. Remove from heat and serve with garnishes.
