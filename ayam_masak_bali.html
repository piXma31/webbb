Ayam Masak Bali Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces as desired
  • Turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 8 stalks lemongrass, bruised
  • 6 sprigs curry leaves
  • 1 tablespoon ground chili
  • 3 tablespoons sweet soy sauce
  • Cooking oil
  • Gula Melaka or granulated sugar
  • A little water

Ingredients for blending:

  • 5 large onions
  • 10 shallots
  • 10-12 cloves garlic
  • 2-inch ginger
  • 1-inch belacan (shrimp paste)

How to Cook:

  • Mix the chicken with turmeric powder and salt. Fry in hot oil until halfway cooked.
  • In the same oil (reduce if too much), approximately 1 cup, sauté lemongrass and curry leaves until fragrant.
  • Add ground chili and blended ingredients. Cook until the oil separates.
  • Put in the chicken, soy sauce, sugar, and salt to taste. Pour in a little water and stir slowly until the gravy thickens. If the gravy is too dry, add a little more water.
  • Once the gravy thickens and the oil separates, remove from heat and serve.
